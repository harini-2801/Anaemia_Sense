ℹ️ About Anaemia Sense

Anaemia Sense is an advanced predictive screening system that utilizes machine learning classifiers to assess the risk of anemia based on complete blood count (CBC) parameters. By analyzing the relationship between hemoglobin levels and red blood cell sizing indexes (MCV, MCH, MCHC), the system screens for anomalies with high precision.

🧬 Understand the Parameters

Hemoglobin: Low values are the primary indicator of anemia.

MCV: Sizing indicates whether anemia is microcytic (small cells, e.g., iron deficiency) or macrocytic (large cells, e.g., B12/folate deficiency).

MCH & MCHC: Chromicity metrics indicating the density of hemoglobin inside the cells, helping distinguish different subtypes of blood disorders.
